About me

Hello, I am Tejasvi Chabbra, a fourth-year undergraduate student pursuing a double major in Economics and Electronics and Communication at BITS Pilani. My academic pursuits reflect my deep interest in the field of finance, which I have been actively exploring through my studies and research projects.

Over the years, I have had the opportunity to work on several exciting projects related to finance. Under the guidance of Dr. Nivedita Sinha at BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus, I am currently working on a project investigating the impact of bank consolidation on lending relationships in the Indian context. Additionally, I am performing Econometric time series analysis using various models under the supervision of Dr. Rishi Kumar to explore the link between financial markets and the real economy in India.

I have also gained practical experience through my internships in Investment Banking and Venture Capital, which further ignited my interest in pursuing research in finance. With a strong foundation in economics and a keen eye for detail, I aspire to contribute to the advancement of the field through my work.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me and my interests.
